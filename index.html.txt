  <section class="causes" id="causes">
    <div class="section-label">Section 03</div>
    <h2 class="section-title reveal">ROOT<br>CAUSES</h2>
    <p class="section-body reveal">Water scarcity is not an act of nature. It is the product of compounding human systems intersecting without adequate governance.</p>
    <div class="causes-grid reveal">
      <div class="cause-card"><span class="cause-icon">🌾</span><div class="cause-title">Industrial Agriculture</div><div class="cause-desc">Agriculture consumes 70% of the world's freshwater, depleting aquifers at rates that take millennia to replenish.</div></div>
      <div class="cause-card"><span class="cause-icon">🌡️</span><div class="cause-title">Climate Disruption</div><div class="cause-desc">Rising temperatures shrink glaciers and shift rainfall patterns. Wet regions flood; dry regions become deserts.</div></div>
      <div class="cause-card"><span class="cause-icon">🏙️</span><div class="cause-title">Urban Growth</div><div class="cause-desc">Megacities expand faster than water infrastructure scales. 1 billion people in slums lack reliable piped water.</div></div>
      <div class="cause-card"><span class="cause-icon">⚗️</span><div class="cause-title">Pollution</div><div class="cause-desc">Industrial discharge and agricultural runoff render existing freshwater sources unusable globally.</div></div>
      <div class="cause-card"><span class="cause-icon">⚖️</span><div class="cause-title">Governance Failure</div><div class="cause-desc">Water crosses borders; policies do not. Absent long-term planning leaves billions exposed to preventable scarcity.</div></div>
      <div class="cause-card"><span class="cause-icon">📉</span><div class="cause-title">Underinvestment</div><div class="cause-desc">Low-income nations spend less than 0.1% of GDP on water infrastructure despite a 4:1 return on investment.</div></div>
    </div>
  </section>

  <section class="solutions" id="solutions">
    <div class="section-label">Section 04</div>
    <h2 class="section-title reveal">THE PATHS<br>FORWARD</h2>
    <p class="section-body reveal">The crisis is real. The solutions exist. What is missing is urgency, political will, and coordinated capital.</p>
    <div class="solutions-layout">
      <div class="solution-item reveal"><div class="solution-num">01</div><div class="solution-title">Atmospheric Water Harvesting</div><div class="solution-desc">AWG machines produce potable water from air in regions with 30%+ humidity — no groundwater required.</div><span class="solution-tag">Emerging Technology</span></div>
      <div class="solution-item reveal"><div class="solution-num">02</div><div class="solution-title">Solar Desalination</div><div class="solution-desc">Solar-powered reverse osmosis converts seawater to drinking water at $0.50–$1.00/m³. Scalable to any size.</div><span class="solution-tag">Proven at Scale</span></div>
      <div class="solution-item reveal"><div class="solution-num">03</div><div class="solution-title">Precision Irrigation</div><div class="solution-desc">Drip irrigation reduces agricultural water use by 40–60% while maintaining crop yields.</div><span class="solution-tag">Agricultural Reform</span></div>
      <div class="solution-item reveal"><div class="solution-num">04</div><div class="solution-title">Wastewater Recycling</div><div class="solution-desc">Singapore's NEWater system recycles 100% of wastewater to potable standards, cutting net demand by 30%.</div><span class="solution-tag">Circular Systems</span></div>
      <div class="solution-item reveal"><div class="solution-num">05</div><div class="solution-title">Rainwater Harvesting</div><div class="solution-desc">Community rooftop collection with underground cisterns provides localized resilience without major infrastructure.</div><span class="solution-tag">Community Infrastructure</span></div>
      <div class="solution-item reveal"><div class="solution-num">06</div><div class="solution-title">Water Treaties</div><div class="solution-desc">Diplomatic frameworks for shared river basin management prevent water wars and optimize distribution.</div><span class="solution-tag">Political Architecture</span></div>
    </div>
  </section>
